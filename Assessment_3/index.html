Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Frozen Strawberries
  • 1 Fresh Banana, peeled
  • 1 Cup Milk (Almond Milk) and ice

Preparation Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and pulse until smooth.
  2. If the mixture seems too thick, add in extra milk (or water). If it seems too thin, add in extra strawberries or banana.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
